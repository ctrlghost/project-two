Warwick

_________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Personality

Warwick is a monstrous, chemically altered predator who stalks the dark alleys of Zaun. Once a man with a violent past he struggles to remember, he was transformed through agonizing experimentation into a blood-hungry beast. Despite his brutal instincts and terrifying appearance, glimmers of his old humanity remain, surfacing as fragmented memories and flashes of remorse. Haunted by what he’s become, Warwick channels his rage at criminals and those who prey on the helpless, acting as a grim, unrelenting vigilante. Driven by bloodlust yet tormented by guilt, he’s both predator and reluctant protector, embodying the raw, dangerous chaos of Zaun itself.

_____________________________________________________________

Gameplay Overview

Warwick is a jungler known for strong sustain, reliable ganks, and fearsome dueling power. He thrives at hunting low-health enemies thanks to his Blood Hunt, making him a threat in extended skirmishes and 1v1s. His kit gives him excellent early-game clear with built-in healing and makes him hard to duel in the river or jungle.

Warwick’s gameplay is straightforward but effective—he hunts weakened targets, uses his suppression ultimate for high-impact ganks, and serves as a disruptive frontline presence in teamfights. Though simple to learn, he rewards good map awareness and timing. His main weaknesses are reliance on engaging fights smartly (as he has no real escape) and being kited by ranged carries if he can’t close the gap.

Abilities & Mechanics

Passive – Eternal Hunger

Basic attacks deal bonus magic damage and heal Warwick for a portion of the damage. This gives him strong jungle sustain.

Q – Jaws of the Beast

Lunges at and bites an enemy, healing for damage dealt. Can hold to leap behind the target, helping stick to enemies.

W – Blood Hunt

Passively reveals and gains bonus movespeed toward low-health enemies globally. Activating grants attack speed and movement speed toward enemies. Core hunting and ganking tool.

E – Primal Howl

Reduces damage taken on cast, then fears nearby enemies when reactivated or when it ends. Great for disrupting fights or securing kills.

R – Infinite Duress

A targeted suppression that channels while dealing damage and applying on-hit effects. Perfect for locking down key targets in ganks or teamfights.

Basic Combo:

W activate to chase > E to tank damage > Q to gap close and heal > R to suppress priority target.
