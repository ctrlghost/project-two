Ashe

_________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Personality

Ashe is the disciplined and determined warleader of the Freljord’s largest tribe, the Avarosan. Calm, stoic, and burdened by duty, she strives to unite the fractured, warlike clans under a single banner of peace and stability. Though hailed as a figure of destiny—believed to be the reincarnation of a legendary hero—she remains grounded, introspective, and cautious of the weight that legacy imposes. Fiercely protective of her people yet diplomatic and level-headed, Ashe balances her natural compassion with the hard decisions required of a ruler in a harsh, unforgiving land. She is both an inspiring leader and a reluctant warrior, willing to do what is necessary to prevent needless bloodshed and forge a lasting unity for the Freljord.

_____________________________________________________________

Gameplay Overview

Ashe is a bottom-lane marksman known for her powerful utility and consistent damage. Unlike many ADCs with explosive mobility or burst, she excels at kiting and teamfight control thanks to her slows and global ultimate. Her playstyle revolves around poke, zoning, and setting up picks with Enchanted Crystal Arrow.

She is highly reliable and beginner-friendly, but immobile—making positioning and map awareness critical. In teamfights, Ashe shines by slowing enemies for her team, dealing sustained DPS, and engaging from afar with her ultimate.

_____________________________________________________________

Abilities & Mechanics

Passive – Frost Shot

Basic attacks and abilities slow enemies. Critical strikes deal bonus damage but always slow instead of extra crit damage, making her a kiting powerhouse.

Q – Ranger’s Focus

Grants attack speed and turns basic attacks into a flurry of arrows after building Focus stacks. Main DPS ability for all-ins.

W – Volley

Fires a spread of arrows in a cone, damaging and slowing enemies. Great for poke, waveclear, and zoning.

E – Hawkshot

Reveals a large area anywhere on the map. Excellent for scouting objectives, jungle, or catching rotations.

R – Enchanted Crystal Arrow

Global skillshot that stuns the first champion hit (longer stun at longer range) and damages in an area. Core engage and pick tool, with huge teamfight impact.

Basic Combo:

R to engage > W to follow up > Q for all-in DPS.
