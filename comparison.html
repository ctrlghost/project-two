Tips and Tricks for New and Returning Players

_____________________________________________________________

  1. Focus on a Few Champions

    Don’t try to play everyone. Pick 2–3 champs you like per role and learn them well. Mastery matters more than variety at first.

  2. Understand Your Role

    Each role has a clear purpose. Learn the basics:

    • Top: Bruiser or tank, side-lane control.
    • Jungle: Objective control, ganking.
    • Mid: Burst damage, roaming.
    • ADC (Bot): Consistent damage.
    • Support: Vision, peel, engage.

  3. Last-Hitting is Key

    CS (creep score) wins games. Practice last-hitting in custom games. 10–15 extra CS = ~1 kill’s gold.

  4. Map Awareness Wins Games

    Check the minimap every few seconds. Look for missing enemies, approaching junglers, and objectives.

  5. Ward More Than You Think

    Vision saves lives. Always carry and place Control Wards. Replace trinkets wisely (yellow → blue or red).

  6. Don't Fight Without a Reason

    Random skirmishes often lose games. Fight for objectives, vision control, or numbers advantage.

  7. Ping to Communicate

    Use pings! Danger, Missing, On My Way, Assist Me. It’s the easiest way to coordinate with random teammates.

  8. Learn Cooldowns

    Know your champion’s cooldowns—and your enemies’. Punish them when their big spells are down.

  9. Don't Spam Chat Negatively

    Flaming never helps. Focus on your own play. Mute toxic players if needed.

  10. Play Around Objectives

    Kills help, but dragons, Rift Herald, Baron, and towers win games. Prioritize them.

  11. Adjust Your Builds

    Don’t auto-build the same items every game. Adapt based on matchups and enemy composition.

  12. Respect Enemy Jungle Timers

    If you don’t see the enemy jungler on the map, assume they’re near you. Ward, play safer, or call it out.

  13. Watch High-Level Play

    YouTube guides, streamers, or pro matches can teach you better macro, positioning, and decision-making.

  14. Stay Calm When Behind

    You can win from behind with good teamfights and objective steals. Don’t tilt, don’t surrender too early.

  15. Have Fun and Take Breaks

    League is intense. Take breaks if you’re tilted or tired. Play with friends to make it more enjoyable.
