Jayce

_________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Personality

Jayce is a brilliant inventor and proud defender of Piltover, celebrated as a hero for his technological genius and unshakable commitment to progress. Confident—some might say arrogant—he believes deeply in protecting his city from any threat, no matter the cost. Though charming and charismatic, Jayce can be impulsive and dismissive of those he sees as obstacles to his vision of Piltover's future. He values results over caution, often clashing with colleagues who question his methods. At heart, Jayce is driven by a genuine desire to safeguard Piltover’s ideals and people, even as his uncompromising nature sometimes alienates those closest to him.

_____________________________________________________________

Gameplay Overview

Jayce is a top-lane (or sometimes mid-lane) fighter known for his versatile, lane-dominant playstyle and ability to switch between melee and ranged forms. He excels at bullying opponents early with long-range poke and strong all-in potential, making him a lane bully who can snowball hard.

His ranged Cannon Stance lets him poke safely and siege, while Hammer Stance offers burst damage and mobility for all-ins. Jayce rewards aggressive, precise play and strong understanding of trading windows. However, he’s vulnerable to being outscaled by tanky opponents if he falls behind, and lacks hard CC for teamfights beyond his knockback.

_____________________________________________________________

Abilities & Mechanics

Passive – Hextech Capacitor

Switching stances grants a brief movement speed boost, aiding in trading, chasing, or escaping.

Q – To the Skies! / Shock Blast

•Hammer Stance: Leap to an enemy, dealing AoE damage and slowing.

•Cannon Stance: Fires a long-range shot that can be accelerated for huge poke damage.

W – Lightning Field / Hyper Charge

•Hammer Stance: Passive deals AoE damage; active deals AoE around him.

•Cannon Stance: Supercharges next 3 attacks with rapid fire.

E – Thundering Blow / Acceleration Gate

•Hammer Stance: Knocks back and damages target.

•Cannon Stance: Creates a gate that accelerates Shock Blast projectiles and boosts ally move speed.

R – Mercury Cannon / Mercury Hammer

Switches between ranged Cannon and melee Hammer forms, refreshing his abilities and adapting his combat style on the fly.

Basic Combo:

Cannon E + Q for long-range poke > switch to Hammer > Q to gap-close > W and E for burst.
